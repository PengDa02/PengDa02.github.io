MOMENTS WORK ABOUT ME

I am Da Peng, a PhD student at Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Wei Ke. My research primarily focuses on UMMs and MLLMs. I am broadly interested in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I believe that bridging the gap between vision, language, and other modalities is crucial for the next generation of general-purpose AI. My long-term goal is to explore simple, scalable, and effective architectures for multimodal intelligence.

I was fortunate to work with many exceptionally talented researchers. These include Zonghao Guo, Yansong Li, Shichu Sun, Jiale Wang, Xuesong Yang, and Yichen Zhang. More will be added here over time.
